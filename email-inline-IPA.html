Thanks for choosing to be a beta tester for myCuratio!

To begin testing, you first need to install myCuratio on your iPhone. You will find the application file attached, and instructions for how to install it below.

Click to watch a short training video

Follow these steps to intall the IPA (shown in the video above):

  1. Connect your phone to iTunes
  2. Dowload IPA File
  3. Double click on the IPA to add it to iTunes
  4. Open iTunes
  5. Navigate to the Apps screen
  6. Add myCuratio to your device
  7. Sync your device

Please contact us if you need assistance installing myCuratio.
